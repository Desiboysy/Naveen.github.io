What will be the challenges to industry for not being on cloud computing platform

  1. Industries had to face Security Challenge wothout Cloud Computing.

  2. As CRM has been the best in order to handle Customer Relation Management Servics without Salesforce it becomes Challenging
  for the Industries to Ineract with the Customers.

  3. As in Manufacturing Industry CRM in Salesforce keeps Track of Contacts ,Deals, manufacturing ,Shipping etc. without Cloud Computing it willbe challenging
  for Manufacturing Industries to keep Track of   their Customers Contacts,Deals,Shipping etc.

  4. In Banking and Finance Services Cloud Computing helps to keep Track of  Transactions , Amount of Transaction ,
  At what time Transaction Happened ,  they need to know who is the the buyer , who is the   sender ,
  who is the lender etc these are the Details which are needed to keep Track by Banking and Finance Industries So,
  without Cloud Computing it becomes difficult for the Industry to Manage these   Details.
  Sale Cloud , Service Cloud , Marketing Cloud, IOT Cloud, Health Cloud , Commerce Cloud etc of Salesforce.com increases Flexibility in Interacting with Customers in Industries.
